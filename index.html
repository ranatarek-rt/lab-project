what is racism?

Racism, also called racialism, the belief that humans may be divided into separate and exclusive biological entities called races, racism takes many forms and can happen in many places. It includes prejudice, discrimination or hatred directed at someone because of their colour, ethnicity or national origin.

Racism can be most simply understood as someone behaving differently to another person based on the colour of their skin or culture, people who look different or speak a different language and people wear certain styles of clothing because of their religion and may get bullied because of this

Racism is more than just words, beliefs and actions. It includes all the barriers that prevent people from enjoying dignity and equality because of their race.
